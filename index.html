Leicester City: An Improbable Story

With four weeks left to the English Premier League season, Leicester City are in the prime position to be crowned as champions with a five point lead ahead of second place Tottenham. The Premier League at a Glance

The Premier League is the top division of English Football consisting of 20 teams. At the conclusion of every season, the lowest three teams are relegated to a lower division, while three teams from the Championship, the division just below, are promoted to the EPL. Add text visualization here.

Here, are the standings for the last ten seasons of the English Premier League. In the Premier League, a win awards three points, a draw one, and a loss zero points. After 38 weeks of gameplay, the team with the highest points is crowned the champion. Ties are broken by goal differential, the number of goals scored minus the number of goals conceded.
